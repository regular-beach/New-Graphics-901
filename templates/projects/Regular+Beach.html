This project started as a idea for a typeface I was sketching while traveling. The letters were then carved from linoluem, often changed from the original sketch on the go and sometimes recarved more than once. I was experimenting with letterpress printing and t-shirts and decided to start a project to combine all of my different art and design interests into one effort with Regular Beach fronting as a sort of collective/media library.
